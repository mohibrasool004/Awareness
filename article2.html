Understanding Encryption: A Simple Guide

Encryption is a process that converts data into a code to prevent unauthorized access. It is widely used to protect sensitive information from cyber threats. There are various encryption methods, each with its level of security.

For instance, AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) is widely used for its strength and efficiency. Understanding how encryption works can help you appreciate its role in keeping your data safe.
