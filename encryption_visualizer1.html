What is Encryption?

Encryption is a process of converting plain text into a coded format to prevent unauthorized access. It is used to protect sensitive data during transmission or storage.

Examples of Encryption

  • AES Encryption: Encrypts data using a symmetric key algorithm.
  • RSA Encryption: Uses a pair of keys, one for encryption and one for decryption.
